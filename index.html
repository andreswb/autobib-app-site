Features

Everything an enthusiast needs to catalogue a collection.

Scan the info card

Photograph the placard at a show. AutoBib reads the make, model and year on-device and finds the exact match in the catalogue.

On-device & private

No accounts, no cloud, no tracking, no ads. Recognition runs offline and your collection never leaves your iPhone.

Your garage, organised

Separate cars you own from cars you've spotted, mark favourites, and keep a wishlist of the ones you're chasing.

Rarity & condition

Rate rarity on a transparent 0–100 scale, from Common to Grail, and grade condition on the collector 1–5 scale — including the German Zustandsnote.

Clubs & events

Track your own club and others, log the shows and meets you attend, and tag the cars you spotted at each one.

A growing catalogue

Hundreds of classics and modern enthusiast cars are built in. Additional curated collections can be unlocked with optional in-app purchases.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

AutoBib does not collect, transmit, or process any personal data. There are no accounts and no servers of our own.

  • Everything you create — photos, car records, clubs, events and notes — is stored only on your device and never leaves it.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.
  • The only optional network feature is a “look up extra details” button that fetches a public Wikipedia summary using a car’s model name. It includes no personal data and never runs on its own.
  • In-app purchases are processed by Apple; we never receive your payment details.

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to share, sell, or delete on our side. To remove all app data, simply delete the app.
